Code Book 

This is the code book for the data produced by the program titled run_analysis.R.


Data file resulting from running run_analysis.R:

MeasurementMeansByActivitySubject.txt


Variables within the aforementioned text file include the subject, the activitylabel and the means of the remaining measurements and statistics which are listed below.  All measurements below subject and activitylabel have been normalized between -1 and 1 and are therefore unitless:

activitylabel - a label for the activity conducted; labels included were: walking, walkingupstairs, walkingdownstairs, sitting, standing and laying

subject - an integer from 1 to 30 representing the person from whom the measurements were taken

tbodyaccmeanx - mean of the body's acceleration along the x-axis as a function of time

tbodyaccmeany - mean of the body's acceleration along the y-axis as a function of time

tbodyaccmeanz - mean of the body's acceleration along the z-axis as a function of time

tbodyaccstdx - standard deviation of the body's acceleration along the x-axis as a function of time

tbodyaccstdy - standard deviation of the body's acceleration along the y-axis as a function of time

tbodyaccstdz - standard deviation of the body's acceleration along the z-axis as a function of time
